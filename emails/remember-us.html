What is Entre?

01

The perfect NYC restaurant finder for your friend group.

Entre figures out where everyone can actually get to — taking into account where each person is coming from, not just whoever's loudest in the group chat.

02

No more "idk, what do you want?"

The app factors in what everyone is craving, price range, time constraints, and dietary restrictions, then gives you a refined answer, not a list of 40 options to argue about.

03
